# Real Device QA

Date: 2026-05-17

## iPhone Safari Checklist

Priority: highest.

- Open preview URL in Safari.
- Test at top of page, mid page, contact form, FAQ, final CTA, and footer.
- Confirm sticky CTA respects bottom safe area.
- Confirm sticky CTA does not cover important footer/final CTA content.
- Confirm no horizontal scroll.
- Confirm urgency ticker is clipped and does not widen the page.
- Confirm smooth scroll to contact works from hero CTA.
- Confirm sticky CTA button scrolls to contact.
- Confirm phone link opens call prompt.
- Confirm form fields are visible and tappable.
- Do not submit the form.

## Android Chrome Checklist

- Open preview URL in Chrome.
- Confirm no horizontal scroll.
- Confirm sticky CTA appears after scroll.
- Confirm CTA buttons remain tappable.
- Confirm form fields are visible and tappable.
- Confirm no visual jumps while scrolling.
- Do not submit the form.

## Desktop Chrome Checklist

- Open preview URL in Chrome.
- Confirm desktop layout parity.
- Confirm sticky mobile CTA is hidden visually on desktop.
- Confirm floating call button behavior after scroll.
- Confirm nav hide/show behavior on scroll.
- Confirm form layout is intact.
- Confirm FAQ accordions are usable.
- Confirm no console errors.
- Do not submit the form.

## Evidence To Record

- Device/browser name.
- Preview URL.
- Date and tester.
- Screenshots of hero, contact form, sticky CTA, footer.
- Any console errors.
- Any horizontal scroll or overlap issues.
